I'm Andrea Lynn, somatic coach, embodiment guide, and keeper of sacred space for women who know, deep in their bones, that they're meant for more.

My work lives in the body. In the breath. In the places you've stopped feeling, the desires you've stopped voicing, the version of yourself you've been quietly shelving.

I use somatic process, breathwork, feminine embodiment practices, and guided visualization to help women move from numb to alive, from performing to becoming.

This isn't therapy. It's not conventional coaching. It's initiation. And it changes everything.
