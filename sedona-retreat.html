Why Retreat With Me

A reset your body can actually keep.

Retreats with me are designed to shift you out of your normal patterns and into a more coherent state, where your body, mind, and direction start working together again. We choose locations intentionally, places where the natural environment supports that shift, not just visually but energetically.

Each day blends movement, breathwork, and time in nature with real space to slow down, reflect, and connect, both with yourself and with a group that's also there for something deeper. There is an element of ceremony and reverence woven throughout, not as performance, but as a way to anchor the experience.

You are not just stepping away from your life. You are experiencing what it feels like to live with more clarity, presence, and connection, and leaving with a body and awareness that can actually sustain it.

The Land That Holds Us

Held by Sedona's red rock canyons.

Nestled along the flowing waters of Oak Creek, Briar Patch Inn offers a serene sanctuary on nine lush acres, where water, stone, and sky conspire to slow time. Wander shaded paths, gather in quiet nooks, rest beside the creek, or immerse yourself in the simple medicine of being held by nature.

You will rest in historic 1940s cabins thoughtfully adorned with Southwestern décor and Native American art. Each space honors simplicity, comfort, and a sense of place. Private patios invite you to step outside, breathe in the fresh air, and listen to the creek's quiet song.
