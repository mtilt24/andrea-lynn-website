A Sacred Return

Slow down. Breathe with the land. Remember who you are.

This is an invitation to come home to yourself, unhurried, supported, and deeply nourished. Set on nine lush acres where water, stone, and sky conspire to slow time, Briar Patch Inn is a living oasis. Wander shaded paths, gather in quiet nooks, rest beside the creek, and immerse yourself in the simple medicine of being held by nature.

A Message from Andrea

These red rocks stole my heart more than twenty years ago.

Sedona is one of my most favorite places on Earth. My intention is to support you in coming home to your body as a portal between the cosmos and the Earth. Together, we co-create a container where you can expand your awareness, anchor your dreams into reality, and remember who you are.

Your Facilitators

Guided by Andrea & Rose.

Two devoted teachers holding space for remembrance, renewal, and return.

Initiatory Guide & Somatic Coach

Andrea Lynn

An alchemist of the heart and a tuning fork for Gaia's communication, Andrea is an initiatory guide and somatic teacher devoted to supporting people through pivotal thresholds of transformation. Her work weaves embodied practice, mythic inquiry, and nervous-system awareness, so that change is integrated not just conceptually, but through lived experience in the body. She has spent over two decades guiding students through yoga, retreat work, teacher trainings, and one-on-one mentorship.

Yoga & Buti Yoga Instructor, IAYT

Rose West

A dynamic and gifted instructor, Rose trained through the Omni Flow Life Alchemy Program, Yoga Alliance, and The Stress Management Center of Marin, and is a certified Buti Yoga instructor. With more than twenty years in health and wellness and thirteen years of nutrition coaching, she creates pathways to healing through breath, asana, mantra, mudra, Ayurveda, shaking, tapping, and meditation.
