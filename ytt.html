Yoga Teacher Training

Teach from your whole self.

Twenty years of teaching yoga and leading trainings, distilled into a progression that develops both highly skilled teachers and deeply embodied practitioners.

A 200-hour foundation and a 300-hour deepening. Clarity, somatic embodiment, and the capacity to lead real change through the work.

Hero · Andrea Teaching

My Approach

Beyond a certification.

After 20 years of teaching yoga and leading trainings, these programs have evolved beyond a standard certification. They are designed to develop both highly skilled teachers and deeply embodied practitioners. People who can not only lead a class, but create meaningful change through the work.

This work integrates technical teaching skill, somatic embodiment, and personal transformation. You're not just learning information. You're training your ability to use the body as a tool for awareness and change, to work with different students in real time, and to develop presence, clarity, and authority as a teacher.

We go beyond minimum certification standards to create an immersion that changes how you practice, how you teach, and how you relate to others.

The Progression

Two trainings. One arc.

The 200-hour builds the foundation. The 300-hour refines and deepens it. Take one, or follow the full path.

200hr · Training Group

200-Hour Training

Learn to Teach. Cross the Threshold.

From practitioner to teacher.

The emphasis is on teaching skill and applied understanding, so you leave able to lead real classes with confidence.

You'll Develop

  • Strong foundations in asana, alignment, and intelligent sequencing
  • Clear, effective cueing for different bodies and experience levels
  • Practical experience in teaching and holding space
  • Consistency in your own practice to support what you're teaching

Format

Begins November 7. Meets every other weekend over 12 weeks, plus a 4-day immersion retreat.

Practice · Embodiment

What You'll Train

Skills you can actually use.

You're not just learning information. You are training your ability to lead, to read a room, to teach from your body, and to hold space when it matters.

  • Use the body as a tool for awareness and change.
  • Understand how to work with different students in real time.
  • Develop presence, clarity, and authority as a teacher.
  • Build a deeper relationship with your practice and the people you're working with.

Why This Training

By the end, you won't just understand yoga. You'll be able to teach it.

In a way that's clear, safe, and impactful. With a body that knows what it is asking of others. With the presence to hold a room and the discernment to meet who's actually in it.

This is not about adding more information. It's about becoming more effective, more intuitive, and more grounded in your work.
