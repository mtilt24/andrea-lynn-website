The Process

InVision Coaching.

InVision is how I guide women out of the inherited story and back into their own authorship.

Most women I work with already know what is not working. The marriage that flattens them. The career that fits like someone else's coat. The voice inside that keeps whispering there is more.

What fewer women know is what they actually want. Or how to trust themselves enough to move toward it.

Together we clear the subconscious patterns that have been running the show. We re-establish the foundation your life is built on. And from that ground, we begin building what you are here to embody: the relationships, the work, the body, the aliveness.

You stop living as a reaction to what happened to you. You become the Creatrix of what happens next.
